About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I received my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong in 2024, and was fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Hong Xu and Prof. Chun Jason Xue.

My research interests generally lie in computer networking and systems and especially in: high-performance network stacks, programmable network devices, and storage systems. Currently, I work on hardware-software co-design and network devices for large ML models.
