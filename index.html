About Me

I am a 4th-year Ph.D. student from Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), advised by Prof. Hong Xu from The Chinese University of Hong Kong and Prof. Chun Xue from City University of Hong Kong. I received my B.E. in Computer Science and Technology from Northeastern University (NEU) in 2018.

I am enthusiastic about computer networking and systems, particularly data center networking. My current research projects focus on exploiting hardware (FPGAs, DPUs, etc.) to reduce communication overhead and accelerate data center workloads, e.g., NFV, microservices, and big data analytics.
