About

Qiang Su (苏强) is a 3rd-year Ph.D. student in computer science at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), supervised by Prof. Chun Xue from CityU and Prof. Hong Xu from CUHK. He received his B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Northeastern University (NEU) in 2018. He is enthusiastic about computer networking and systems, particularly data center networks. His current research project is to exploit hardware (FPGAs, SmartNICs, etc.) to reduce communication overhead and accelerate data center workloads, e.g., NFV, microservices, and big data analytics.
